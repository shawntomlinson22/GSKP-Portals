Link Help (SharePoint / GSKP)
  1. If a click opens blank or does nothing: right-click the link → Open link in new tab.
  2. If you see a download prompt, choose Open (browser preview) when available.
  3. If SharePoint opens a sandboxed preview, use the new tab workflow to bypass the preview frame.
Shortcut: hold Ctrl (or Cmd) while clicking to force a new tab in most browsers.
